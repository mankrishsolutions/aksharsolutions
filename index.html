Akshar LMS (Lead Management System)

IndiaMART LMS

We understand the importance of efficient lead management in driving business growth. Akshar Lead Management System helps businesses automatically download, organize, and manage leads in a structured system so that no opportunity is missed.

Key Features

  • Centralized Lead Database Management
  • Automatic download of IndiaMART Direct Leads
  • Download Buy Leads and Mobile Call Leads

IndiaMART Leads Data Excel Add-In

Effortlessly manage your IndiaMART leads data with our versatile Excel Add-In. This powerful tool enables businesses to download leads directly from IndiaMART's API and organize them within Excel for easy access and analysis.

Key Features

  • Seamless lead download - Automatically download your IndiaMART leads data directly into Excel, saving time and reducing manual effort.
  • Data validation - Ensure accuracy and consistency with built-in validation checks that help identify and fix data issues.
  • Text search & special character removal - Easily search for specific information and clean up data by removing unwanted special characters.
  • Data management - Maintain and organize your leads data in a structured, user-friendly format for streamlined processing.
